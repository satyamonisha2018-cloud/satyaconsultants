FAQ

Common questions before you connect.

Do you help freshers?

Yes. We support students, freshers, and job seekers based on available company openings and profile fit.

How can companies share hiring requirements?

Companies can use the enquiry form or contact Satya Consultants directly with role, location, salary range, and joining timeline.

Which roles do you support?

We can support IT, BPO, customer support, HR, sales, operations, data entry, banking, and similar business roles.

How does candidate matching work?

We review basic details such as education, skills, location, communication, availability, and interest before connecting suitable profiles.
